About SUNWELL GROUP

SUNWELL GROUP LTD. operates as a trust sales platform, integrating resources across the entire construction, decoration, and home furnishing industry chain. We have established a comprehensive service platform that combines exhibition, transaction, logistics, and settlement services.

We are deeply involved in Ukraine's post-war residential and public facility reconstruction plans and special reconstruction projects, actively cooperating with the Ukraine Reconstruction Committee to recommend high-quality and affordable building materials to US-Canada-EU aid projects for Ukraine.

Our Organizational Structure

  • Platform Operations: SUNWELL GROUP is responsible for overall project management, platform operations, and cross-border coordination, directly interfacing with the Ukraine Reconstruction Committee and reconstruction project builders
  • Storage & Display Centers: Located in Kyiv, Odessa, Lviv, and Zaporizhzhia regions
  • Dream Rebuild Volunteers Association (DRVA): Provides free reconstruction design solutions for our self-built projects
  • Dreams Realized Charity (DRC): Fundraises for reconstruction projects

Platform Unique Advantages

Multi-Channel Sales Integration: We provide complete sales channel solutions including display platform, retail platform, wholesale platform, and special supply platform - a four-in-one approach. Our storage and display centers serve as both product storage bases and customer-facing exhibition and transaction venues.

Project Procurement Guarantee: Secured procurement orders from Ukraine reconstruction projects (in close cooperation with Ukraine Post-War Reconstruction Committee, Charity Foundation, and Volunteer Association), our self-built project procurement orders, and special supply orders for Ukraine special reconstruction projects. We ensure every qualified supplier has opportunities to receive procurement orders.

Cost Optimization: Suppliers only need to bear reasonable storage and platform operation fees to enjoy exhibition, sales, and delivery services, saving the huge investment of building their own sales networks and exhibition centers.

Market Opportunities

Ukraine Reconstruction Market Scale: The total reconstruction market is estimated at over $524 billion, covering residential, public facilities, and infrastructure needs. The reconstruction period spans 10-15 years, providing long-term revenue opportunities for suppliers.

Policy Support Advantages: Our platform has obtained direct approval from the Ukraine Reconstruction Committee, enjoys green procurement channels and tariff reduction policies, and has significant advantages in local price competition.

Win-Win Cooperation Model

Through this innovative cooperation model, SUNWELL GROUP not only provides sales channels for suppliers but also offers a complete overseas market expansion solution, enabling building material suppliers to enter the international reconstruction market and our proprietary project market with minimal investment.
